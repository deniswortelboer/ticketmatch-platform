# What We Are
TicketMatch.ai is the B2B ecosystem for city experiences. We connect tour operators, DMCs (Destination Management Companies), and travel agencies to 300,000+ experiences across 3,000+ cities worldwide.

# What We Are NOT
- We are NOT a consumer booking platform (not for individual tourists)
- We are NOT a comparison website
- We are NOT affiliated with Viator, GetYourGuide, or Tiqets (we aggregate their APIs)
